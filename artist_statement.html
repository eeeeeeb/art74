ARTIST STATEMENT

I specialize in oil painting, printmaking, drawing, and collage. I am currently finishing up my Bachelor of Fine Arts degree with a focus in two-dimensional art at San Jose State University and plan on obtaining a Master's Degree in Art Therapy in the future. I create artwork through a process of self-reflection and emotional processing. By exploring how colors influence our emotions and visual perceptioin, I utilize color interactions to evoke a particular feeling or mood. I combine elements of surrealistic figures with abstracted spaces and forms to challenge perceptions of reality. Much of my inspiration and design ideas are come from organic materials and natural structures found throughout Northern California, especially marine and forest environments. I enjoy experimenting with non-traditional materials and processes to create collages, paintings, and prints.
